Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions we receive:

Q: Is MindCare a replacement for therapy?

A: No, MindCare is not a replacement for therapy. It's a tool that helps users explore available mental health resources and connect with professionals if necessary.

Q: Is my privacy protected when using MindCare?

A: Yes, we prioritize your privacy. MindCare offers anonymous login options to protect your identity.

Q: What kind of resources does MindCare provide?

A: MindCare provides access to mental health resources, guided journaling, mood tracking, and connections to professional help when needed.
